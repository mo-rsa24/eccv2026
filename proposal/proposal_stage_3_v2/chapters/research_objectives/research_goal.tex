\section{Research Goals}
\label{sec:research_objectives_research_goal}

The primary research objectives are as follows:

\begin{enumerate}

    \item \textbf{Characterise the Composability Gap.}
    Measure how severely logical composition diverges from semantic composition, and determine whether this gap varies systematically across concept pairs grouped by co-occurrence support, feature-space disentanglement, or semantic conflict, establishing it as a predictable property of concept pairs rather than a set of isolated failure cases.

    \item \textbf{Determine the Cause of the Gap.}
    Identify whether the gap stems from how concepts are represented in the latent space or from the compositional method used at inference time, thereby locating the main bottleneck to reliable compositional generation.

    \item \textbf{Demonstrate Practical Utility in Medical Imaging.}
    Test whether compositional generative models can produce plausible rare co-morbid presentations at inference time, without joint training examples, demonstrating that the earlier insights are practically useful in low-resource clinical settings.

\end{enumerate}
