\section{Mathematical Preliminaries}
\label{sec:math_prelim}

This section establishes the theoretical foundation underlying diffusion-based generative modelling, emphasizing how probability densities evolve under deterministic and stochastic dynamics. The concepts presented here—spanning the continuity equation, differential equations, and their probabilistic reformulations—form the mathematical core of the compositional generative diffusion framework proposed in this study.
