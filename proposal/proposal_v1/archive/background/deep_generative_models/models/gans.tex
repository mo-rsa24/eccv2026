\paragraph{Generative Adversarial Networks.}
In parallel, Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) \citep{NIPS2014_f033ed80} reframe generative modeling as a two-player game between a generator $G_\theta(z)$ and a discriminator $D_\phi(x)$. The classic GAN objective is
\begin{equation}
\min_\theta \max_\phi \, \mathbb{E}_{x \sim p_{\text{data}}} [\log D_\phi(x)] + \mathbb{E}_{z \sim p(z)} [\log (1 - D_\phi(G_\theta(z)))].
\end{equation}
