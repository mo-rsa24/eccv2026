\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:research_objectives_conclusion}
This chapter has defined the research goals and hypotheses that guide the thesis. Together, they move from establishing the composability gap, to identifying its cause, to testing whether the same principles can enable clinically meaningful out-of-distribution generation in chest X-rays. The next chapter develops the methodological framework used to evaluate these claims.
