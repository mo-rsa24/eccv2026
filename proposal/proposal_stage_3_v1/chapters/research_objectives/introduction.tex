\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:research_objectives_introduction}
The preceding chapter established the motivation for the proposed research by outlining the limits of current compositional generative models and the practical significance of overcoming them. Building on that foundation, this chapter sets out the aims of the study by defining its research goals and hypotheses. Together, these provide the conceptual structure that guides the remainder of the thesis.
