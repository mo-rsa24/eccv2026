\chapter{Background}
\label{chap:background}

This chapter provides the technical foundations a reviewer needs to follow the three-phase
research programme. Section~\ref{sec:vae_disentanglement} covers variational autoencoders
and disentangled representation learning, which underpins Phase~2. Section~\ref{sec:diffusion_models}
introduces diffusion models and score-based generation, which underpins all three phases.
Section~\ref{sec:compositional_diffusion} presents the compositional diffusion framework ---
the product-of-experts operator, its theoretical validity conditions, and why representational
factorisation determines whether composition succeeds --- which motivates Phases~1, 2, and~3.
Section~\ref{sec:medical_imaging_background} covers chest radiography generation and
domain-specific encoders relevant to Phase~3.
